Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

OKO-TO is a protected payment link product for WhatsApp and Instagram sellers in Nigeria. We are currently in early access and building toward a full product launch. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

When we refer to "OKO-TO," "we," or "us" in this document, we mean the team behind the OKO-TO product.

What information we collect

Right now, the only way to interact with OKO-TO is through our waitlist form. When you fill in that form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your business category (what you sell)
  • Your Instagram handle, if you choose to share it

We do not collect payment card details, bank account information, or any financial data at this stage. We do not track cookies or use advertising pixels on this site.

How we use your information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Contact you on WhatsApp when your early access is ready
  • Understand which seller categories to prioritise during our early launch
  • Send updates about OKO-TO that are directly relevant to your waitlist position

We do not sell your information to anyone. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes. We will not send you messages unrelated to OKO-TO.

Where your data is stored

Your waitlist information is stored in Supabase, a cloud database service. Supabase stores data on secure infrastructure and complies with standard data protection practices. You can read more about Supabase's security approach at supabase.com.

We do not store your data in any other system at this time.

How long we keep your data

We keep your waitlist information for as long as OKO-TO is in development and for a reasonable period after launch. If you ask us to delete your details, we will do so promptly. See the section below on your rights.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct any information that is wrong
  • Ask us to delete your information from our records
  • Opt out of further communications from us at any time

To exercise any of these rights, send us a message on WhatsApp or Instagram and we will respond within a few days.

Third-party links

Our pages may link to external websites such as Instagram and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. Please check their own policies before sharing personal information with them.

Changes to this policy

As OKO-TO grows, this policy will be updated to reflect new features and legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. If you are on our waitlist, we may also notify you directly.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your information, you can reach us through our Instagram page or by sending a WhatsApp message to the number we use for seller outreach.

We are a small team and we take these questions seriously. You will get a real response.
